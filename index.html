MEET THE TEAM

At WK Tattoo, we create custom tattoos and professional piercings in a clean, welcoming studio. Whether you're planning your first tattoo or adding to your collection, our artists and resident piercer are here to bring your vision to life.

Meet our Artists

Kai

Owner & Tattoo Artist

As the owner of WK Tattoo, Kai specializes in Traditional Japanese, Chinese-inspired designs, anime, watercolor, and fine line tattoos. Whether you’re looking for bold Japanese sleeves, custom anime pieces, or delicate fine line work, Kai creates tattoos that blend strong artistry with lasting quality.

Abel specializes in black and grey realism and portrait tattoos. From lifelike portraits to detailed memorial pieces, he focuses on creating realistic tattoos with depth, precision, and lasting impact.

Everett

Tattoo Artist

Everett specializes in black and grey realism, color realism, and neo traditional tattoos. His versatile style allows him to create everything from vibrant color pieces to bold, highly detailed realism.

Sara specializes in black and grey tattoos, lettering, fine line work, chicano, and anime. She creates clean, detailed tattoos ranging from delicate script and fine line designs to custom black and grey inspired pieces.
