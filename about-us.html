ABOUT WK TATTOO

At WK Tattoo, we turn your ideas into art that lasts. Located in the heart of Metairie, LA. Our artists works in a variety of styles, including black and grey realism, fine-line, and traditional Japanese tattoos.

We focus on creating a clean, safe, and welcoming space where you can feel comfortable from start to finish. Every design is custom and made to reflect your story and your style.

Whether it’s your first tattoo or you’ve been collecting for years, we’re here to make the process easy, personal, and something you’ll be proud to wear. Let’s create something meaningful together.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide high-quality tattoos and professional piercings in a clean, safe, and welcoming environment while creating artwork that reflects each client’s story, individuality, and vision. We are committed to exceptional craftsmanship, genuine connections, and an experience that clients can trust from start to finish.

Vision

To be a trusted destination for custom tattoos and professional piercings in the Greater New Orleans area, recognized for creativity, quality, and a client-first experience. We strive to build a community where artists and clients can collaborate to create meaningful work that lasts a lifetime.
