ABOUT WK TATTOO

At WK Tattoo, we turn your ideas into art that lasts. Located in the heart of Metairie, LA. Our artists works in a variety of styles, including black and grey realism, fine-line, and traditional Japanese tattoos.

We focus on creating a clean, safe, and welcoming space where you can feel comfortable from start to finish. Every design is custom and made to reflect your story and your style.

Whether it’s your first tattoo or you’ve been collecting for years, we’re here to make the process easy, personal, and something you’ll be proud to wear. Let’s create something meaningful together.
